Spaghetti bolognese

Description

A delicious mix of pasta and beef. This configuration will have you frothing at the mouth.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • tomato paste
  • cheese

Steps

  1. whack spaghetti into boiling water
  2. brown ground beef on a frying pan and add tomato paste
  3. Sprinkle cheese like salt bae
