Lasagna

Description

A delectable beef lasagna with a sprinkling of parmesan cheese. The rich tomato sauce and pastry will send tingles down your spine when you eat this delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Pastry
  • 500g ground beef
  • tomato paste
  • cheese

Steps

  1. whack pastry into pan
  2. brown ground beef on a frying pan and add tomato paste
  3. whack ground beef into pan and add layers of ground beef
  4. Sprinkle cheese like salt bae
